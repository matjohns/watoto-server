Watoto

An interactive medical calculator implementing Kenyan basic paediatric protocols.

Designed for use by trained medical staff; Watoto supports Kenyan clinicians to assess nutritional status, approximate certain anatomical attributes and calculate essential drug dosing for paediatric patients; quickly and easily, whilst helping to reduce the likelihood of human error.

Install

As Apple have unfortunately rejected the app so far, the only way to currently install is via ad-hoc builds. However we will need to register your iPhone's UDID before the app will install.

This method is only intended for use by RCPCH volunteers who have an iOS device, rather than mainstream distribution.

UDID

This is the developer identifier for your phone, We can register up to 100 devices to our developer account and produces builds for them.

You can get your UDID from iTunes, or via the convenient get.udid.io website.
